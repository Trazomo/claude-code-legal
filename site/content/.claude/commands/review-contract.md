---
description: Structured contract review with risk analysis
---

Review the contract at $ARGUMENTS and provide a structured analysis:

## 1. Parties
Identify all parties, their roles (buyer/seller, licensor/licensee), and any guarantors.

## 2. Key Terms
Extract and summarize:
- **Duration**: Initial term, renewal provisions, notice periods
- **Value**: Total contract value, payment schedule, late payment penalties
- **Termination**: For cause, for convenience, material breach definition
- **Renewal**: Auto-renewal terms, opt-out window

## 3. Risk Flags
Rate each provision: Low / Medium / High / Critical
- Indemnification scope and caps
- Liability limitations (or lack thereof)
- IP assignment or license grants
- Non-compete or non-solicitation clauses
- Data handling and privacy obligations
- Governing law and dispute resolution

## 4. Missing Clauses
Check for the absence of:
- Indemnification
- Limitation of Liability
- Confidentiality / NDA
- Dispute Resolution
- Force Majeure
- Assignment restrictions

## 5. Recommendations
Provide a prioritized list of negotiation points, ordered by risk level.

Format the output as a structured markdown report with a risk summary table at the top.
