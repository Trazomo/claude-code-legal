# Contract Review Conventions

These rules load automatically when reviewing or editing contract files.

## Defined Terms
- Verify every defined term (capitalized) has a definition section
- Check that defined terms are used consistently throughout
- Flag any term used but not defined, or defined but not used

## Cross-References
- Verify all section cross-references point to the correct section
- Check that "as defined in Section X" references are accurate
- Flag circular references

## Standard Clauses Checklist
Every commercial contract must include:
- [ ] Indemnification (mutual, with gross negligence / willful misconduct carve-outs)
- [ ] Limitation of Liability (capped at 12 months' fees)
- [ ] Confidentiality / NDA provisions
- [ ] Governing Law and Dispute Resolution
- [ ] Force Majeure (including pandemic, cyber, government action)
- [ ] Assignment and Change of Control
- [ ] Termination and Survival

## Red Flags
Flag immediately if you find:
- **Unlimited liability** for either party
- **Broad IP assignment** (beyond project deliverables)
- **Non-standard governing law** (anything other than NY)
- **Unilateral amendment rights**
- **Auto-renewal without notice** period under 60 days
- **Waiver of jury trial** without client consent

## Ambiguity
- Mark language that could be interpreted against the client
- Note where "reasonable" or "material" is used without definition
- Flag provisions where obligations are unclear or contradictory
